==========================
$setIsSubset (aggregation)
==========================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. expression:: $setIsSubset

   .. versionadded:: 2.6

   Takes two arrays and returns ``true`` when the first array is a
   subset of the second and ``false`` otherwise.
